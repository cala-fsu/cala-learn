Florida Teacher Certification Examinations (FTCE)

CALA has worked with thousands of prospective teachers and listened to their requests related to FTCE test preparation. As a result, we designed these learning modules to help candidates get one step closer to certification.

Each module features

  • learning activities designed by test development experts
  • alignment with the Florida Department of Education competencies and skills to help you analyze your performance on each item and determine what you need to study further

Learn the FTCE General Knowledge Reading Test with these practice items

The Reading module is designed to give you practice on the knowledge of key ideas and details, craft and structure, and integration of information and ideas based on text selection.

Coming Soon! The General Knowledge ELS Practice Module

The English Language Skills module is designed to give you practice on the knowledge of language structure, vocabulary application, and standard English conventions.

CALA is an applied research center at Florida State University with a mission to raise and assess the level of learning in all settings. Located in Tallahassee's premier research and development complex, CALA resides at the hub of knowledge, alongside more than 40 high-tech and research-based facilities.

We embrace a holistic approach to instruction, learning, and assessment. Our goal is to help our clients consider the entire cycle of learning without sacrificing any one element. To that end, we collaborate with them to set goals, develop research-based training and assessment tools, and design platforms for effective presentation of information.
