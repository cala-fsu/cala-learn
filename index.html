Florida Teacher Certification Examinations (FTCE)

CALA has worked with thousands of prospective teachers and listened to their requests related to FTCE test preparation. As a result, we designed these learning modules to help candidates get one step closer to certification.

Each module features

  • learning activities designed by test development experts
  • alignment with the Florida Department of Education competencies and skills to help you analyze your performance on each test question and determine what you need to study further

General Knowledge Reading Module

The Reading module gives you an opportunity to practice on competencies such as knowledge of key ideas and details, craft and structure, and integration of information and ideas based on text selection.

Coming Soon! General Knowledge ELS Module

The English Language Skills module gives you an opportunity to practice on competencies such as knowledge of language structure, vocabulary application, and standard English conventions.

CALA is a research and development center at Florida State University, responsible for initiatives that improve assessment and learning in Florida and beyond.

To accomplish our mission, we embrace a holistic approach to instruction, learning, and assessment. Our goal is to help our clients consider the entire cycle of learning without sacrificing any one element. To that end, we collaborate with them to set goals, develop research-based training and assessment tools, and design platforms for effective presentation of information.
